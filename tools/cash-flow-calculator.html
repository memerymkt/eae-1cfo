Cash Flow Calculator: Optimize Business Liquidity

Cash flow management is critical for business survival and growth. This calculator helps CFOs monitor, forecast, and optimize cash flows to ensure liquidity and financial stability. Understanding cash flow patterns enables proactive decision-making and prevents cash flow crises that can threaten business operations.

Operating Cash Flow Calculation

Operating cash flow measures core business cash generation:

Operating Cash Flow = Net Income + Depreciation + Amortization - Changes in Working Capital

Focus on cash flows from primary business activities excluding financing and investing activities.

Free Cash Flow Analysis

Free cash flow represents cash available for growth and returns:

Free Cash Flow = Operating Cash Flow - Capital Expenditures

FCF shows true cash profitability after maintaining operations and assets.

Use FCF for valuation and investment analysis.

Cash Flow Ratios and Metrics

Key metrics for cash flow analysis:

Cash Flow Margin = Operating Cash Flow / Revenue

Cash Flow Coverage = Operating Cash Flow / Debt Service

Free Cash Flow Yield = Free Cash Flow / Enterprise Value

Cash Burn Rate = Monthly negative cash flow

Monitor these metrics for liquidity and solvency assessment.
